Direct Preference Optimization for Diffusion Models
Jan 16, 2024

DPO aims to overcome the primary drawbacks of RLHF, which are it’s unstable nature and the dependence on a reward model trained on human preference data. In this paper, they perform DPO on diffusion models by training the model to alter it’s denoising directions towards preferred images over non-preferred ones. For this objective, they use the pick-a-pic v2 dataset. I’ll be using this script as a reference. Note that in the aforementioned paper, they finetune the entire model but in the script we see that they’re fine-tuning a LoRA adapter on the model.
